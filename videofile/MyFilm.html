TV Shows

These series have captivated me with their complex characters and compelling narratives.

Action

The Flash

Creator: Greg Berlanti, Andrew Kreisberg, Geoff Johns

Stars: Grant Gustin, Candice Patton, Danielle Panabaker

Network: The CW

Years: 2014-2023

The Flash is an American superhero television series based on the Barry Allen incarnation of DC Comics character the Flash, a costumed superhero crime-fighter with the power to move at superhuman speeds. After being struck by lightning, Barry Allen awakens from his coma to discover he's been given the power of super speed, becoming the next Flash, fighting crime in Central City.

Daredevil

Creator: Drew Goddard

Stars: Charlie Cox, Vincent D'Onofrio, Deborah Ann Woll

Network: Netflix

Years: 2015-2018

A blind lawyer by day, vigilante by night. Matt Murdock fights the crime of New York as Daredevil, a hero with heightened senses who delivers his own brand of justice in Hell's Kitchen. The series is known for its gritty tone and exceptional fight choreography.

Drama

Breaking Bad

Creator: Vince Gilligan

Stars: Bryan Cranston, Aaron Paul, Anna Gunn

Network: AMC

Years: 2008-2013

A high school chemistry teacher diagnosed with inoperable lung cancer turns to manufacturing and selling methamphetamine in order to secure his family's future. Breaking Bad follows Walter White's transformation from a mild-mannered teacher to a fearsome drug kingpin.

Stranger Things

Creator: The Duffer Brothers

Stars: Millie Bobby Brown, Finn Wolfhard, Winona Ryder

Network: Netflix

Years: 2016-Present

When a young boy vanishes, a small town uncovers a mystery involving secret experiments, terrifying supernatural forces, and one strange little girl. Set in the 1980s, the series combines supernatural elements with nostalgic references to pop culture of the era.

K-Drama

Crash Landing on You

Director: Lee Jeong-hyo

Stars: Hyun Bin, Son Ye-jin, Seo Ji-hye

Network: tvN, Netflix

Years: 2019-2020

A South Korean heiress accidentally crash-lands in North Korea while paragliding. There, she meets a North Korean officer who decides to help her hide. This romantic drama explores themes of love that transcends political boundaries.

Sci-Fi

Black Mirror

Creator: Charlie Brooker

Stars: Various (Anthology series)

Network: Channel 4, Netflix

Years: 2011-Present

An anthology series exploring a twisted, high-tech multiverse where humanity's greatest innovations and darkest instincts collide. Each episode presents a standalone story with unique characters and settings, examining modern society through the lens of technology.

Westworld

Creators: Jonathan Nolan, Lisa Joy

Stars: Evan Rachel Wood, Jeffrey Wright, Ed Harris

Network: HBO

Years: 2016-2022

A dark odyssey about the dawn of artificial consciousness and the evolution of sin. Set at the intersection of the near future and the reimagined past, it explores a world in which every human appetite can be indulged without consequence.
