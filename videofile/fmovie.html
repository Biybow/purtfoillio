My Favorite Movies

Cinema has the power to transport us across time and space, showing us worlds beyond our imagination and reflecting our own reality in profound ways.

Action Comedy Drama Sci-Fi Thriller

Action

John Wick

Director: Chad Stahelski

Stars: Keanu Reeves, Michael Nyqvist, Alfie Allen

Released: 2014

Runtime: 101 minutes

An ex-hit-man comes out of retirement to track down the gangsters that killed his dog and took everything from him. John Wick revitalized the action genre with its stylish choreography, world-building, and minimalist storytelling.

Comedy

Superbad

Director: Greg Mottola

Stars: Michael Cera, Jonah Hill, Christopher Mintz-Plasse

Released: 2007

Runtime: 113 minutes

Two co-dependent high school seniors are forced to deal with separation anxiety after their plan to stage a booze-soaked party goes awry. This coming-of-age comedy captures the awkwardness and intensity of teenage friendships with both hilarity and heart.

Ang Pangarap kong Holdap

Director: Marius Talampas

Stars: Paolo Contis, Pepe Herrera, Jerald Napoles, Jelson Bay

Released: 2018

Runtime: 107 minutes

Set in the imaginary Barangay Husay, Eman teams up with his two friends, Toto and Carlo who start as the underdogs, failing at every opportunity to succeed in their small-time scams. They then meet Nicoy who becomes part of Eman's group breaking the tradition of having only 3 members per gang.

Sci-Fi

Kokey

Director: Romy Suzara

Stars: Jerry Lopez Sineneng, Ani Pearl Alonzo, Mahal

Released: 2010

Runtime: 148 minutes

On their way home, Bong and Anna discover Kokey, an extraterrestrial whose spaceship exploded upon landing on earth. Without their parents' knowledge, the children keep Kokey at home.The three become the best of friends and Kokey turns out to be a big help to the family and their business, attracting customers using his extraterrestrial powers. But Kokey becomes homesick, pointing to the sky in silent tears, missing his parents. He tries to contact them but has no success.
